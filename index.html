Classic Margherita Pizza

Introduction:

Margherita pizza is a classic Neapolitan pizza known for its simplicity and Fresh flavours.Named after Queen Margherita of Italy,it features the colours of the Italian flag red tomatoes,white mozzarella and green basil.This recipe will guide you through making a delicious Margherita pizza from scratch.

Ingredients:

For the dough:
-2 1/4 teaspoons(1 packet) active dry yeast
-1 cup warm water(110F or 43C)
-1 teaspoon granulated sugar
-2 1/2 cups all-purose flour
-2 tablespoons olive oil
-1 teaspoon salt

For the toppings:

-1/2 cup homemade or store-bought pizza sauce
-8-10 fresh basil leaves
-11/2 cups fresh mozzarella cheese,sliced or shredded
-2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
-1/2 teaspoon salt
-1/4 teaspoon black pepper
-1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

Dough Preparation:

1.In a small bowl,combine the warm water and sugar.Sprinkle the yeast over the mixture,stir gently and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
2.In a large mixing bowl,combine theflour and salt.Make a well in the centre and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.Stir until the dough comes together.
3.Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it's smooth and elastic.Form it into a ball and place it in a lightly oiled bowl.Cover with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
4.Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can reach(usually around 500F or 260C).If you have a pizza stone,place it in the oven while it preheats.
5.Roll out the risen dough on a floured surface into a circle or rectangle,depending on your preference.If you're using a pizza stone,you can shape it directly on a piece of parchment paper foe easy transfer.

Topping and Banking:

1.Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough,leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
2.Place the fresh mozzarella slices or shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
3.Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the mozzarella.
4.Sprinkle salt,black pepper and optionally some crushed red pepper flakes over the pizza.Drizzle the olive oil evenly on top.
5.If using a pizza stone,carefully transfer the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the hot stone in the oven.If not using a stone,simply place the pizza on a baking sheet.
6.Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
7.Carefully remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute.Slice it,and it's redy to serve.

Tips

-Customize your pizza with additional toppings like pepperoni,olives,mushrooms or any of your favorite ingredients.
-Feel free to use a pizza peel to transfer the pizza to a hot pizza stone,if available.
